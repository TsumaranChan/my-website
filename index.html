About SunRise Eikaiwa

A school built on real conversation

Founded in 2010, SunRise Eikaiwa was born from a simple belief: the best way to learn English is to actually use it. Every class is designed around natural communication — not textbook drills.

Our small-group classes (max 6 students) mean you get plenty of speaking time, personal feedback, and a relaxed, judgement-free environment. Whether you're a shy beginner or preparing for TOEIC, we have a class for you.

  • ✓Native English-speaking teachers from the US, UK, Canada, and Australia
  • ✓Maximum 6 students per group class
  • ✓In-person (Yokohama) and online lessons available
  • ✓Free trial lesson for new students
